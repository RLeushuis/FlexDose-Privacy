# FlexDose-Privacy
Privacy Policy for FlexDose - A local-first medication tracking app that prioritizes your privacy. All data stays on your device.

 # Privacy Policy for FlexDose

  **Last Updated: November 2025**

  ## Overview

  FlexDose is a medication tracking application designed with privacy as a core principle. This privacy policy
  explains how FlexDose handles your information.

  ## Our Privacy Commitment

  **FlexDose does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers.** All your information stays
   exclusively on your device.

  ## Information Storage

  ### What Data is Stored Locally

  FlexDose stores the following information **locally on your device only**:

  - **Medication information**: Names, dosages, units, intervals, start times, and maximum doses per day
  - **Dose history**: Records of when you took your medication
  - **Personal notes**: Any notes you add to your medications
  - **App preferences**: Theme colors, language settings, button styles
  - **Remaining medication counts**: Optional tracking of your medication supply

  ### Where Your Data is Stored

  All data is stored locally using AsyncStorage on your iOS device. This data:
  - Never leaves your device
  - Is not transmitted to any servers
  - Is not accessible by FlexDose developers
  - Is protected by your device's security features
  - Is deleted when you uninstall the app

  ## Features and Privacy

  ### Notifications

  FlexDose uses local notifications to remind you about your medication:
  - All notifications are scheduled locally on your device
  - No notification data is sent to external servers
  - Notification permissions are requested when you first use the app
  - You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings

  ### Data Export

  FlexDose offers optional data export functionality:
  - You can export your data to CSV or JSON format
  - Export is **completely optional** and user-initiated
  - Exported files are saved to your device or shared via iOS share sheet
  - You have full control over what you do with exported data
  - No data is automatically uploaded or shared

  ### No Analytics or Tracking

  FlexDose does NOT use:
  - Analytics services
  - Crash reporting tools
  - Usage tracking
  - Advertising networks
  - Third-party SDKs (except Expo Notifications for local notifications)

  ## Third-Party Services

  FlexDose uses the following third-party services, all of which operate locally:

  - **Expo Notifications**: Used to schedule local notifications on your device. No data is sent to Expo servers.
  - **React Native**: The framework used to build the app. No data collection.

  ## Your Rights and Control

  You have complete control over your data:

  - **Access**: All your data is visible within the app
  - **Edit**: You can modify any medication or dose record at any time
  - **Delete**: You can delete individual records or clear all data via Settings
  - **Export**: You can export your data at any time
  - **Portability**: Exported data can be imported into other apps or systems

  ## Children's Privacy

  FlexDose does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data stays
   on the device, there are no age restrictions from a privacy perspective.

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at
  the top of this document. Continued use of FlexDose after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

  ## Data Security

  While FlexDose does not transmit your data, we recommend:
  - Using device passcode/biometric protection
  - Keeping your iOS device updated
  - Being cautious when exporting and sharing data

  ## Legal Compliance

  ### GDPR Compliance

  Since FlexDose does not collect or process personal data on external servers, most GDPR requirements do not apply.
   However, we respect the spirit of GDPR by:
  - Providing full transparency about local data storage
  - Giving users complete control over their data
  - Enabling easy data export and deletion

  ### Apple App Store Compliance

  This app complies with Apple's App Store privacy requirements:
  - No data collection or tracking
  - Local storage only
  - Transparent about notification usage
  - Clear explanation of file sharing (export functionality)

  ---

  **Summary**: FlexDose is a privacy-first app. Your medication data never leaves your device, and you maintain
  complete control over your information at all times.
